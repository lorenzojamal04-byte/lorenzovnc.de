Case Study — Theater Festival 2025

A Performance
Across Cultures

In July 2025, the Konfuzius-Institut Frankfurt presented a landmark cultural event: the Zaifen Huangmei Theater Ensemble from Anhui Province, led by Han Zaifen — one of China’s most decorated performers and a celebrated star of the Huangmei Opera tradition.

Huangmei Opera traces its roots to songs sung by women during the tea harvest in Anhui. Distinct from Peking Opera and Yue Opera in its melodic character and emotional directness, it was inscribed as part of China’s National Intangible Cultural Heritage in 2006.

Presenting a 30+ person ensemble of this standing to a German audience requires more than logistics — it requires understanding the cultural weight of what is being offered, and ensuring the production honours it. I played a key supporting role in the production: assisting with artist arrival logistics, scheduling coordination, and on-site backstage operations across the three-day programme.
